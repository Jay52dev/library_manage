Library Terms & Conditions

1. Library Membership

All users must register to borrow books. Members should provide valid identification and keep their credentials confidential.

2. Borrowing Policy

Each user can borrow up to 3 books at a time for a period of 14 days. Late returns may incur a fine of $0.50 per day.

3. Book Care & Responsibility

Borrowers are responsible for maintaining books in good condition. Damaged or lost books must be replaced or paid for.

4. Library Conduct

Users should maintain silence and respect library rules. Any disruptive behavior may result in membership suspension.

5. Digital Resources

Access to digital resources is restricted to members only. Sharing credentials is strictly prohibited.

6. Privacy & Data Protection

Personal data collected during registration is confidential and will not be shared without user consent.

7. Amendments

The library reserves the right to update these terms as needed. Changes will be communicated via official channels.
